Introduction to Neurosketch

At Neurosketch, we are transforming the way Parkinson's disease is detected. By combining artificial intelligence with medical expertise, we offer a cutting-edge, accessible tool that allows individuals to get an early and accurate diagnosis right from home. Our mission is to empower people with the knowledge they need to take control of their health and seek timely medical intervention.




Our Mission

Our mission is to revolutionize Parkinson's disease detection through AI, making early diagnosis accessible to everyone, anywhere in the world.

Our Vision

Our vision is a world where neurodegenerative diseases are diagnosed early, improving outcomes for millions through AI-powered solutions.

Our Story

At Neurosketch, our mission is deeply personal. The inspiration behind this project stems from witnessing a loved one struggle with Parkinson's disease.

"My grandfather was a strong, vibrant man, but when he started showing signs of Parkinson's, it took far too long for a proper diagnosis. The uncertainty and the delay in treatment weighed heavily on our family, and I often wondered if there was a way to detect the disease earlier. It was in those moments of helplessness that the idea for Neurosketch was born. I didn’t want other families to go through the same pain of not knowing."

Neurosketch was created with the goal of providing individuals with a powerful tool to detect Parkinson's disease early—before symptoms become overwhelming. By harnessing the power of artificial intelligence, we aim to give people the knowledge and peace of mind they need to take control of their health sooner. This project is not just about technology; it’s about bringing hope to those who need it the most, inspired by a journey that began in our own family.
